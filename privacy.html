1. What We Collect

Server IDs, channel IDs, user IDs, message IDs, and timestamps for core functionality.

2. How We Use It

Data is used exclusively for Bot operation: config, moderation logs, giveaways, tickets, and auto-moderation.

Data is stored locally in SQLite. No data is shared or sold to third parties.

4. Retention

Data is kept while the Bot is in your server. Request deletion by contacting the owner.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your data. Removing the Bot stops data collection.
