Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Hinako stores the following data: server IDs, channel IDs, user IDs, message IDs, and timestamps. This data is necessary for the Bot's core functionality.

2. How We Use Information

All collected data is used exclusively for operating the Bot's features: server configuration, moderation logs, giveaway management, ticket system, and auto-moderation filters.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the server hosting the Bot. No data is transmitted, shared, or sold to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained while the Bot remains active in your server. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the bot owner.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, modification of, or deletion of your stored data. Removing the Bot from your server will stop all data collection.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the bot owner directly on Discord.
