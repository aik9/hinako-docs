Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Hinako stores: Server IDs, channel IDs, user IDs, message IDs, and timestamps for configuration and moderation purposes.

2. How We Use Information

All data is used exclusively for Bot functionality: server config, moderation logs, giveaways, tickets, and auto-moderation.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in a local SQLite database. No data is transmitted to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained while the Bot is active in your server. Request deletion by contacting the bot owner.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your stored data. Removing the Bot from your server stops all data collection.

6. Contact

Contact the bot owner on Discord for privacy inquiries.
